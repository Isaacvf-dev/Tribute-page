Mel and Lux

The adventure dogs

Here's a timeline of Mel and Lux's lifes:

  • 2018 - Mel is born in Fortaleza, Ceará
  • 2019 - Mel is adopted by Isaac and Pedro and she moves to Sobral, Ceará.
  • 2020 - Mel moves to Canindé, Ceará with her family. It's where Lux is born and adopted to be Mel's sister.
  • 2023 - Mel and Lux live with their family, Isaac ad Pedro in Canindé, Ceará. They love playing with their toys and going out for walks.
